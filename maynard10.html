INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTER

SARAH

RIVERA

I still remember August 27, 2025, a Wednesday that completely changed my definition of “unexpected.” There she was Sarah Jean Rivera in a yellow outfit so bright, even the sun looked jealous. It was during the CCIS General Assembly, but honestly, from the moment I saw her, it felt like the whole event was just our background music. Who knew that the girl who accidentally made me forget what the program was about would later become my girl best friend my partner in crime, my emotional damage consultant, and the only person who can make me laugh even when I’m mad at her. Since that day, every memory with Sarah feels like a rom-com scene… except ours doesn’t end when the credits roll.
